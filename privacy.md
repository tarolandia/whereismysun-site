## What data the App uses

### Location
The App requests access to your device's GPS location. Your coordinates are used solely to calculate the sun's position in the sky for your location. This calculation happens entirely on your device. Your location is never stored, never transmitted to any server, and never shared with any third party.

### Camera
The App accesses your device's camera to display a live AR overlay of the sun's path. The camera feed is processed in real time on your device. No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.

### Device sensors
The App reads your device's compass, accelerometer, and gyroscope to orient the AR overlay. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never stored or transmitted.

### No account or registration
The App requires no account, login, or registration of any kind. No personal information is collected through a registration process.

### No tracking, no analytics, no ads
The App does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or any tracking technology. No usage data, device identifiers, or IP addresses are collected.

## Data retention

Because the App processes all data on-device and transmits nothing to external servers, the Service Provider holds no personal data about users. There is nothing to retain, delete, or export on the Service Provider's side.

The only data stored on your device is your calibration preference (a compass offset saved in local app storage). This is not personal data and never leaves your device. It is deleted when you uninstall the App.

## Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact support@whereismysun.app and it will be deleted promptly.

## Your GDPR rights

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

- **Right of access** — request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to rectification** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure** — request deletion of your personal data
- **Right to restrict processing** — request that processing be limited
- **Right to data portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Right to object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to withdraw consent** — withdraw consent at any time

Because the App collects no personal data on the Service Provider's servers, most of these rights are satisfied by default. For any request, contact support@whereismysun.app. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority. Contact details: [https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en](https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en)
